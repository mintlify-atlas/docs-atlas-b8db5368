---
title: "Managing your profile"
description: "View and update your participant profile and change your password."
---

The profile page is available at `/profile` when you are logged in as a **PARTICIPANTE**. It lets you view your account information, update your personal details, and change your password.

## What you can see

The profile page header displays your full name (as an avatar with initials) and your email address.

Below the header, a summary row shows:

| Section | Description |
|---|---|
| **Inscripciones** | Total number of competitions you are enrolled in. |
| **Documento** | Your document type and document number (e.g., `DNI · 12345678`). |
| **Miembro desde** | The date your account was created. |

The **Datos personales** section shows your current first name, last name, and email address.

The **Inscripciones recientes** section shows your three most recent enrollments. Each entry displays the tournament name, competition name, and final price. Click **Ver todas →** to go to the full `/inscriptions` page.

<Info>
  Document type and document number are read-only and cannot be changed after registration.
</Info>

## Updating your personal details

You can update your first name and last name. Email is shown but cannot be changed through the profile form.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the profile page">
    Navigate to `/profile`. The page loads your current data from:

    ```http
    GET /api/v1/participantes/me
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Editar">
    In the **Datos personales** section, click the **Editar** button in the top-right corner. The read-only view is replaced by an editable form with **Nombre** and **Apellido** fields pre-filled with your current values.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update your name or surname">
    Edit the **Nombre** and/or **Apellido** fields. Both fields accept letters only (including Spanish accented characters) and must be between 3 and 50 characters.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Guardar cambios**. The button shows a spinner and the label **Guardando...** while the request is in progress.

    The API call made is:

    ```http
    PUT /api/v1/participantes/me
    ```

    ```json
    {
      "firstName": "Juan",
      "lastName": "García"
    }
    ```

    On success, a toast notification confirms **Perfil actualizado correctamente.** and the form returns to read-only view with the updated values.

    If the update fails, a toast notification displays the server error message.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Cancel without saving">
    Click **Cancelar** at any time to discard your changes and return to the read-only view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Changing your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the password section">
    Scroll to the **Cambiar contraseña** section on the `/profile` page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your current password">
    Type your existing password in the **Contraseña actual** field. This is required to verify your identity before the change is accepted.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter and confirm your new password">
    Type your new password in the **Nueva contraseña** field. A strength indicator is displayed below the field.

    Retype the new password in the **Confirmar nueva contraseña** field.

    <Warning>
      The new password must be at least 8 characters. If the two new password fields do not match, an error is shown and the request is not sent.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Cambiar contraseña**. The button shows a spinner and the label **Cambiando...** while the request is in progress.

    The API call made is:

    ```http
    PATCH /api/v1/participantes/me/password
    ```

    ```json
    {
      "currentPassword": "oldpassword",
      "newPassword": "newpassword"
    }
    ```

    On success, a toast notification confirms **Contraseña cambiada correctamente.** and all three password fields are cleared.

    If the current password is incorrect, the server returns an error message displayed in a red alert above the form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile response fields

The `GET /api/v1/participantes/me` endpoint returns:

| Field | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `id` | `Long` | Your participant account ID. |
| `firstName` | `String` | Your first name. |
| `lastName` | `String` | Your last name. |
| `email` | `String` | Your email address. |
| `documentType` | `String` | Your document type (e.g., `DNI`). |
| `documentNumber` | `String` | Your document number. |
| `totalInscripciones` | `int` | Total number of your enrollments. |
| `createdAt` | `LocalDateTime` | Account creation timestamp. |
